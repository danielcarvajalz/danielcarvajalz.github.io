Welcome

I am a PhD Research Scholar at the Department of Economics of NHH Norwegian School of Economics. I am affiliated to the FAIR - The Choice Lab research group. My research is in experimental and behavioral economics, with a focus on group identity, information processing and belief formation, and the effects of social context on behavior.

I will be on the Job Market in the 2023-2024 academic year.
